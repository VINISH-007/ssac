Mission

The mission of the SAIRAM SDG ACTION CLUB is to inspire the students to promote their ideas in various SDG Goals.



To kindle their thoughts via various fun workshops, rallies, student-oriented activities to encourage them to come forth with their awe-inspiring ideas.



AICTE SPICES is a valuable establishment of AICTE which vows to inspire the youth of the nation to build sustainable and feasible projects and ideas for a better nation.



To energize students club as facilitating entity for pursuit of individual interests, creative work, showcasing talent, networking and teamwork opportunities,social experience; organization and management skills, exposure to professional ethics etc.

Vision

SSAC aims to establish a strong foundation in the young engineers of our nation to guide them to help in the betterment of the country, following the paths of SDG Goals. In these Goals and targets, we are setting out a supremely ambitious and transformational vision.



We envisage a world free of poverty, hunger, disease and want, where all life can thrive. We envisage a world free of fear and violence. A world with universal literacy. A world with equitable and universal access to quality education at all levels, to health care and social protection, where physical,



mental and social well-being are assured. A world where we reaffirm our commitments regarding the human right to safe drinking water and sanitation and where there is improved hygiene; and where food is sufficient, safe, affordable and nutritious. A world where human habitats are safe, resilient and sustainable and where there is universal access to affordable, reliable and sustainable energy.
